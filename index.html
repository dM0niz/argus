Origin

Who is Argus?

In Greek mythology, Argus Panoptes was a guardian with one hundred eyes — able to watch over everything, never sleeping entirely.

ARGUS AI Sentinel transforms this ancient idea into a modern technological network.

Every observation. Every citizen. Every report. Another eye protecting the ocean.

The Challenge

Why ARGUS Exists

Portugal has one of Europe's largest Exclusive Economic Zones. Monitoring such a vast maritime territory is impossible using traditional resources alone.

Thousands of people interact with the ocean every day — fishermen, sailors, whale watching companies, divers, surfers, researchers, beach visitors and families.

Together we can create Portugal's largest citizen-powered ocean monitoring network.

Process

How It Works

From a single observation to national maritime intelligence — every step designed for trust and precision.

1

Citizen submits an observation

A photograph, location and category — from cetaceans to marine litter — sent from the coast.

↓
2

Azure AI Weather enriches the report

Environmental and weather data are automatically attached to every observation.

↓
3

Artificial Intelligence analyses the report

Species identification, classification, deduplication and priority scoring.

↓
4

FrontSea Intelligence Hub organises

Reports are validated, correlated and prepared for operational review.

↓
5

A human operator reviews

Every report passes through human validation before any operational action.

↓
6

Authorities receive information

Scientific partners and authorities receive structured, validated intelligence.

Artificial Intelligence + Human Validation

The AI assists. Humans make operational decisions.

Manifesto

The ocean has never belonged only to sailors.

It belongs to every child playing on the beach. Every fisherman. Every diver. Every scientist. Every citizen who stops to help.

Protecting the ocean begins with observing it.

ARGUS AI Sentinel transforms thousands of individual observations into one national intelligence network.

No satellite can replace the eyes of those who live the sea every day.

Today we are hundreds. Tomorrow we can become thousands.

Every citizen becomes another Eye of Argus.

Together we protect Portugal's greatest natural heritage.
